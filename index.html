About Mentor

Mentor is an application which can help you find your career path. Mentor, as the name suggests, is also where many different mentors can help other in finding their career path. In other words, Mentor allows people who have issues deciding on their career path to get assistance from mentors and people who have also gone through similar situations.

Why use Mentor?

Mentor has a down-to-earth User Interface. Our app is also very simple to handle, making it easy for everyone, knowledgeable, or with little knowledge, to use it without any issues.












The Goal

Our goal is to increase the rate of employment and reduce the inequalities people face when they are selecting their career. With the help of Mentor, people can get advice and help from different mentors, allowing them to unlock secret oppurtunities they didn't know they had, and reduce the inequality of the poor having little to no work options or job oppurtutnities.














The Inspiration

A person goes through a number of phases through their lives. At childhood they are mostly exposed to simple ambitions like an engineer, a doctor and a firefighter. But as they grow, they start developing a passion for something, and then more opportunities for ambitions open up.
